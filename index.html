Kristina's Trail

This project honors Kristina by placing a piece of her in every national park — a rock from her collection and a piece of her for to the wind.

Each one tells a story of her love for nature, adventure, and life.

If you found a rock, feel free to leave a message and help continue her journey.

Submit a Rock Find

If you found one of Kristina’s memory rocks, let us know where and share a story if you’d like. You can upload a photo, too.
